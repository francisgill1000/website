Privacy Policy

Your trust is the foundation of our service. Here's exactly how we collect, use, and protect your data.

Eloquent FZE LLC ("Eloquent", "we", "us", or "our") operates the Eloquent software suite and related services. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our website, mobile apps, and cloud platform.

1 Information We Collect

To deliver a reliable service, we collect the following categories of information:

Account & Contact Information

  • Name, email address, phone number, and company name provided when you sign up or request a demo.
  • Billing details and authorized signatories for paid subscriptions.

Business Data You Upload

  • Customer records, invoices, quotations, inventory, and sales activity created within the platform.
  • Documents and attachments you choose to store with us.

Technical & Usage Data

  • Device type, browser, operating system, IP address, and approximate location.
  • Feature usage, log data, performance metrics, and error reports.
  • GPS location data — only for the Sales Agent App and Live Map Tracker, and only while the app is in use by your authorized staff.

2 How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the Eloquent platform and its features.
  • To process subscriptions, invoices, and customer support requests.
  • To send service announcements, security alerts, and (with your consent) marketing communications.
  • To detect, prevent, and respond to fraud, abuse, or security incidents.
  • To comply with applicable laws and lawful requests from authorities in the UAE.

3 Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only in the limited cases below:

  • Service providers: trusted infrastructure partners (cloud hosting, email delivery, payment processing) under written confidentiality agreements.
  • Your team: data inside your workspace is visible to users you authorize, according to the roles and permissions you assign.
  • Legal compliance: when required by law, court order, or to protect the rights, safety, or property of Eloquent and its users.
  • Business transfers: in the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, with notice to you before your data becomes subject to a different policy.

4 Data Security

We apply industry-standard safeguards to protect your data:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data in transit.
  • Encrypted backups stored daily across geographically separated regions.
  • Role-based access control (RBAC) and audit logs for sensitive operations.
  • Regular security reviews and prompt patching of dependencies.

No system is perfectly secure. If we ever detect a breach that affects you, we will notify you and the relevant authorities without undue delay.

5 Data Retention

We retain your account and business data for as long as your subscription is active. After cancellation, data is retained for up to 90 days to allow for export and reactivation, then permanently deleted from our active systems. Backup copies are purged on our standard rotation cycle (typically within 12 months).

6 Your Rights

Subject to applicable UAE data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal or contractual retention obligations).
  • Export your business data in a portable format.
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@eloquentservice.com.

7 Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses essential cookies to keep you signed in and to remember your preferences. We also use limited analytics cookies (such as session counters) to understand which pages perform well. You can disable cookies in your browser settings; some features may not work as expected if you do.

8 Third-Party Services

Our platform may integrate with third-party services you choose to connect (such as payment gateways, mapping providers, or accounting tools). Those services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them before connecting.

9 Children's Privacy

Eloquent is built for businesses. Our services are not directed to individuals under 18 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. When we make material changes, we will notify you by email or through an in-app announcement at least 14 days before the changes take effect.
